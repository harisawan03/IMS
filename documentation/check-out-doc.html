IT Inventory Management


Checking Items Out


Choosing Check-out Item from the Action page brings you to a simple promting to input the name of the employee receiving the item. It is important that the name is entered exactly as it appears in the database, otherwise there may errors in item tracking. We will go over why this is later.





Hitting Confirm will update the inventory within the database and bring you to a confirmation page. Here you will be ensured the checkout was successful and the data affected will be displayed. From here, you will be able to return to the home page.



Be sure not to go back to check-out page. Doing so may cause discrepencies in database.
