Removing Items from Inventory


The final option for altering the inventory is to remove an item. When doing so, there are two pieces of information that are needed. The amount to remove, pre-populated with "1", and the Reason for Removal. This is required and used for tracking purposes discussed later on.





Hitting Remove will, as expected, redirect you to a confirmation page after updating the database. You will see new quantities for owned and available.

Be sure not to go back to the remove page. Doing so may cause discrepencies in database.

Please be aware, that because of how removal functions internally, if you are letting an employee keep an item they have previously checked out, first check it back in and then and only then should you remove it. Failing to do so will cause the database to be inaccurate!
