IT Inventory Management


Getting Started


Upon arriving at the IMS home page, you will simply see the scanner container with a feed from your device's camera. To begin, place a UPC formatted barcode within this frame. Once the barcode has been detected, you will be automatically routed to the action page.





Here, one of two interfaces will appear. If the UPC scanned is not currently in the database, the following will appear, prompting you to add it to the inventory database.





If it does exist in the inventory, you will see the following, showing current quantities and giving options to check-out, check-in, add to inventory, remove from inventory, or to return to the home page.
